About Me

I'm an art director and designer at The San Diego Union-Tribune. Currently, I work on the Business section and our entertainment weekly tabloid, Night+Day. I collaborate with reporters, editors, graphic artists and photographers to produce visually appealing pages. My work has been recognized by the Society of News with multiple awards of excellence.

I was born in Sarasota, Florida. I'm a graduate of Clemson University in South Carolina with a degree in Marketing. Before moving to San Diego in 2002, I lived in Charlotte, North Carolina for 5 years.

I enjoy running, hiking, long bike rides, listening to music, traveling and drinking wine. I'm also a politics junkie.
